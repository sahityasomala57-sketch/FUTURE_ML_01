Sales & Demand Forecasting Using Tableau

Project Overview

This project analyzes historical sales data from the Superstore dataset and forecasts future sales using Tableau's built-in forecasting capabilities. The dashboard provides a clear view of sales performance and future demand trends.

Forecasting Model

A time-series forecasting model was created using Tableau's forecasting feature based on historical sales data. The model identifies trends in past sales and estimates future sales performance.

Visualizations

The dashboard includes:

- Sales Trend Analysis
- Future Sales Forecast
- Total Sales KPI
- Total Profit KPI
- Total Orders KPI
- Segment-wise Performance Analysis

What the Forecast Means

The forecast represents the expected future sales based on historical sales patterns. It helps identify whether sales are likely to increase, decrease, or remain stable in upcoming periods.

Business Value

A business can use this forecast to:

- Plan inventory levels more effectively
- Reduce stock shortages and overstock situations
- Improve budgeting and financial planning
- Allocate resources efficiently
- Prepare for periods of high or low demand

Key Insights

- Historical sales show recurring trends over time.
- Forecasted sales indicate expected future demand.
- KPI metrics provide a quick overview of business performance.
- The dashboard supports data-driven decision-making.

Conclusion

The forecasting dashboard enables business owners and managers to understand past sales performance and anticipate future demand. These insights can improve planning, operational efficiency, and overall business performance.
